TalenGen Internship

Job Title: Content Creator - Social Media

  • Location : In Person
  • Job Type: Internship
  • Compensation: Unpaid

About TalenGen:

TalenGen strives to be the #1 centralized platform in empowering the next generation of leaders in Southeast Asia through innovative recruitment and professional networking solutions. We aim to bridge the gap between students and employers by building a multilingual recruitment platform that offers multiple features such as AI matching, referral systems, and enhanced security to accommodate diverse cultures and identities.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute a comprehensive content strategy for various social media platforms, aligning with overall marketing goals.
  • Create engaging and visually appealing content, including but not limited to graphics, videos, GIFs, and other multimedia elements.
  • Craft compelling and concise copy for social media posts, captions, and other written content.
  • Foster community engagement by responding to comments, messages, and mentions on social media platforms.
  • Monitor social media analytics to track performance, identify trends, and provide insights for continuous improvement.
  • Contribute to the planning and execution of social media campaigns, contests, and promotions.

Qualifications:

  • Strong creative and storytelling skills with a keen eye for design.
  • Proficiency in social media platforms (e.g., Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn) and scheduling tools.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
  • Knowledge of social media analytics and ability to interpret data.
  • Ability to adapt quickly to changes in social media algorithms and trends.

Why Join Us:

  • Be part of a cutting-edge startup that is reshaping the student recruitment industry in Southeast Asia
  • Collaborate with a talented and diverse team of professionals.
  • Opportunities for career growth and professional development.
